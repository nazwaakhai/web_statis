    • Type

    The Most Popular Type of Clay

    • Type 01

      Polymer Clay

      Clay made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC) or vinyl, plasticizer, pigment, filler, and a little oil. This clay is easy to shape, doesn't dry easily, and is available in various colors. To dry, polymer clay must be baked in the oven.

    • Type 02

      Air Dry Clay

      Clay that hardens in air without needing to be baked. This clay is suitable for DIY projects and home decoration.

    • Type 03

      Light Clay

      Clay is light and easy to shape, ideal for children's crafts.

    • Type 04

      Kaolin Clay

      Mineral deposits also called china clay. Kaolin clay is usually processed into soap, masks and bath bombs

    • Type 05

      DAS Clay

      Clay is a type of paperclay. His character is tough and tough.

    • Chapter 06

      Dough Clay

      This type of clay is very popular among children because the material is soft and safe for children
